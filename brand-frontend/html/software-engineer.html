Software Engineer - Remote

Role Overview

At Eru Labs, we are searching for a Software Engineer passionate about designing, developing, and scaling modern applications to join our dynamic team. Your contributions will directly shape the future of decentralized compute systems and AI-driven platforms.

Responsibilities
  • Develop scalable and efficient backend services for our platform.
  • Collaborate with the product team to understand requirements and create technical solutions.
  • Integrate and manage third-party APIs and tools.
  • Ensure high performance, security, and availability of systems.
  • Contribute to code reviews and improve engineering best practices.
Requirements
  • 8+ years of experience in software development (Python, Go, Rust, or JavaScript preferred).
  • Experience with machine learning training and inference pipelines.
  • Experience with machine learning frameworks (PyTorch, Tensorflow, ect.)
  • Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure).
  • Strong understanding of containerization (Docker, Kubernetes).
  • Proven expertise in distributed system design
  • Familiarity with P2P and meshnet protocols (Bittorrent, BATMAN, ect)
  • Experience with multi platform/multi architecture development.
  • Familiarity with CI/CD pipelines and infrastructure as code (Terraform).
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
Benefits

  • Flexible working hours and remote-first environment.
  • Opportunities for career growth and personal development.
  • Access to cutting-edge technologies and tools.
