Machine Learning Engineer - Remote

Role Overview

At Eru Labs, we are looking for a Machine Learning Engineer who is passionate about creating, deploying, and optimizing advanced machine learning models. Your work will play a crucial role in enhancing our platform's capabilities and revolutionizing decentralized AI systems.

Responsibilities
  • Develop and optimize machine learning models for various use cases.
  • Design and implement scalable machine learning pipelines.
  • Collaborate with data scientists and engineers to build end-to-end solutions.
  • Monitor, evaluate, and improve model performance in production environments.
  • Research and integrate cutting-edge machine learning methodologies.
Requirements
  • 3+ years of experience in machine learning and AI development.
  • Proficiency in machine learning frameworks such as TensorFlow or PyTorch.
  • Strong programming skills in Python and familiarity with libraries like NumPy and Pandas.
  • Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure) and containerization (Docker, Kubernetes).
  • Understanding of data preprocessing, feature engineering, and model optimization techniques.
  • Familiarity with CI/CD pipelines and infrastructure as code (Terraform).
  • Excellent communication and problem-solving skills.
Benefits
  • Competitive salary and equity options.
  • Flexible working hours and a remote-first environment.
  • Opportunities for career growth and personal development.
  • Access to state-of-the-art technologies and resources.
  • A collaborative and innovative team environment.
